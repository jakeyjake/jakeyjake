With a high per-episode price tag, stiff competition from NBC and Fox's strong Thursday night lineups, as well as frequent pre-emptions due to breaking coverage of the Gulf War did not help with ratings. The unusual 8:30 slot did not work and the series floundered when moved to 9pm. 
A brief attempt at rerunning the series in the summer on Fridays in hopes of finding an audience and reversing the cancellation also failed./description
